Part 1 Uninflecting Predicate (UP): predicate that is not inflected for tense or mood.

Part 2 Inflecting Predicate (IP): predicate that is inflected for tense and mood.

Note that labels UP/IP are contentious. Alternative labels would be non-finite verb predicate and finite verb predicate, which reflects Top End language systems better.
